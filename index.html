About Me

Ever since I learned what cars are, I was always facinated about them. When a loud car floored it, I always said," Papa what kind of car is that?" My Dad would answer with the car name. The next question I asked was," Is it a fast car?" My Dad would answer that as well.

When I got to my early teens, I sometimes watched my Dad change the oil on our family car. When my Dad opened the hood, I was there asking what parts do what and their names.A couple days after I turned 13, one of my Dad's workers came over in a Dodge Charger Daytonna. The worker let me sit in the driver seat and floor it. That was one of the best things that happened to me.
